# Faith in Jesus Outreach Ministries Sierra Leone

Official website of Faith in Jesus Outreach Ministries Sierra Leone.

## Motto
"Without Faith It Is Impossible to Please God" (Hebrews 11:6)

## Vision
Our work is worship. Every greeting, every setup, every prayer, and every follow-up matters because people matter to God.

## Mission
We serve with love, lead with integrity, and point every person to Christ.

## Founder & Senior Pastor
Reverend Joshua Umara Tangah

## Address
